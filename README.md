# toy_store_powerbi_project
Power Bi Dashboard Project including creating measures and relational models

Insights from Toy store KPI Report

1.	Overall Business Performance
Total Orders: 829,262
Total Revenue: 14.44M
Total Profit: $4.01M

The business shows strong overall performance with profitability of 28% of total revenue, indicating stable demand across products categories.

2.	Monthly revenue trend
Revenue shows a stable upward trend, with high demand periods:
•	April 2022
•	Stable increase from September 2022 - December 2022
•	March 2023 
These trends are likely correlated with holiday spending, seasonal promotion & high-demand product periods. 
Overall, the business maintains revenue levels between 550K-850K per month, demonstrating consistent customer demand throughout the observed period.

3.	Orders by Product Category
Top 3 categories by order volume
•	Toys – 221,227 Orders
•	Art & Crafts – 220,673 Orders
•	Games – 157,006 Orders 
These three categories make up most of the sales volume indicating strong demand in creative products.
Lower order volume categories
•	Sports & Outdoors – 131,331 Orders
•	Electronics – 99,025 Orders
This indicate that the products from these 2 categories may have higher price and lower demand. 


4.	Profit By Product Category 

Top Profit Contributors
•	Toys – $1,079,527
•	Electronics – $1,001,437
Despite having the lowest number of orders, Electronics contribute almost as much profit as Toys, indicating:
•	High margins
•	Higher price products
•	Strong profitability per transaction

Mid-Tier Profit Contributors
•	Art & Crafts – $753,354
•	Games – $673,993
These categories generate equal volume and profit, showing a healthy balance between demand and profitability.

Lowest Profit Contributor
•	Sports & Outdoors – $505,718
This category may have:
•	Lower margins
•	Seasonal demand
•	Higher operating or cost-of-goods expenses


5.	Key Business Insights

•	Toys are the strongest category, delivering both order volume and profit
•	Electronics offer higher profit despite lower demand, making them high-value category
•	Art & Crafts & Games give balanced performance with both demand and profit
•	Revenue trends highlight seasonal peaks, especially around year-end sales
•	
•	Sports & Outdoors underperform in profit, suggesting an improvement in margin and product mix arrangement



6.	Recommendations
1.	Prioritize Toys and Electronics
These categories deliver the highest impact:
•	Increase inventory availability
•	Expand product range
•	Run category-specific promotions during revenue peak months

2. Observe Pricing Strategy for Sports & Outdoors
•	Review margins
•	Optimize supplier costs
•	Introduce higher-margin items

3. Leverage Seasonal Patterns
Since revenue spikes during certain months:
•	Launch planned promotions ahead of seasonal peaks
•	Allocate more marketing budget during high-demand periods

 4. Explore Electronics Performance
Electronics produce high profit — consider:
•	Expanding into premium electronics categories
